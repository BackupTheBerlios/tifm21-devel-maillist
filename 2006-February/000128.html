That is correct. The SDHCI and Flash Media Controller are completely
seperate interfaces, as is the CardBus Controller and the IEEE 1394
Host Controller. While they both exist in the same chip, interface to
the PCI bus, and share the same physical sockets on most devices,
that's where the similarities end. Unlike the Flash Media Controller,
there is documentation available on the SDHCI, which has enabled the
development of a driver to progress successfully.

The lack of documentation for the Flash Media Controller, and the
complete unhelpfullness of TI has meant that attempts to create a
driver for the interface have had to resort to de-compiling either the
windows drivers (potentially a breach of licensing agreements of the
OS) or the TI binaries that have appeared, which are apparently GPL
anyway.

Having said that, there is work being done on the Flash Media Controller here:

http://tifmxx.berlios.de/
